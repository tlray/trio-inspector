oref Playground experiment

The decision layer of determine-basal.js, re-implemented line-for-line and actually executed in your browser. Pick a real moment from the data as a starting point, then drag the sliders: the code re-runs, values update in place, and branches that are not taken fade out. Forecast values (eventualBG, minPredBG, minGuardBG) are frozen inputs here — in reality they come from the prediction curves. Educational tool; not dosing advice.
